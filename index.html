Wall painting and illustration are visual art forms that involve creating images, designs, and scenes on surfaces such as walls, canvases, or paper. Wall painting focuses on adorning and transforming interior or exterior spaces with intricate, large-scale artwork, often conveying a specific theme, mood, or message

Illustration, on the other hand, encompasses a wide range of styles and techniques used to depict concepts, stories, or ideas through drawings, paintings, or digital media


Both forms of art contribute to aesthetic enrichment and communication of ideas through the creative use of visuals.

Wall painting, or mural art, involves creating visual compositions directly on walls. It's a form of artistic expression that transforms spaces, conveying messages, culture, and aesthetics. Utilizing various techniques and mediums, wall painting adds vibrancy and uniqueness to both indoor and outdoor environments.
